A Day In ISO

Posted on 16/09/2020

Like all of us we have had to deal with a change of routine and habits, that sometimes are scary. But today I would like to share with you a little about what happens on my average school day. So without further ado, here we go!

7am = This is when I make a start to my day and wake up. I would head straight to the bathroom then do my usual bathroom rituals.

7:30am = Brekky time. Usually I have good old oats, but right now I just have some toast to get me started

7:50am = After I've eaten, brushed my teeth and gotten changed, I go for a walk with my doggie Benji.

8:30am = From now til 2:55pm I have school plus a few breaks here and there. (Mostly involving having tea and eating!).

2:55pm = Now this is my chill time and time to have a snack, finish off any homework I may have, text friends and do some hobbies.

6pm = Once ive had a shower its now dinner time and time to chill on the sofa and watch some TV!
